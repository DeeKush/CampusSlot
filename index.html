Resource Booking Agreement

By booking a resource on CampusSlot, you agree to the following terms:

1. Proper Usage
  • Use the resource only for its intended purpose
  • Do not share or transfer your booking to others without permission
  • Arrive on time for your booking slot
2. Care and Maintenance
  • Handle all resources with care and respect
  • Report any damage or malfunction immediately
  • Do not attempt to repair or modify resources yourself
  • Return resources in the same condition as received
3. Liability
  • You are responsible for any damage caused during your booking period
  • Repair or replacement costs may be charged to you
  • Repeated violations may result in booking privileges being revoked
4. Cancellation Policy
  • Cancel bookings at least 2 hours in advance if you cannot attend
  • No-shows may result in penalties or restrictions
  • Repeated cancellations may affect future booking eligibility
5. General Conduct
  • Respect other users and their bookings
  • Keep resource areas clean and organized
  • Follow all campus rules and regulations
  • Do not engage in any prohibited activities
